Exciting news! Proxed.AI now officially supports **Anthropic API proxying**, allowing you to securely integrate models like Claude into your iOS apps.

Route your Anthropic calls through Proxed.AI to gain:

*   **Top-tier Security:** Utilize Apple's DeviceCheck and our Partial Key system for your Anthropic keys.
*   **Centralized Monitoring:** Track usage, manage costs, and get alerts for both OpenAI and Anthropic in one dashboard.
*   **Streamlined Workflow:** Enjoy a unified experience for managing multiple AI providers.

We've added a new Swift code example for the Anthropic proxy in our [Examples section](/). This shows how to call the `/v1/anthropic/<your-project-id>/messages` endpoint.

Explore our [documentation](https://docs.proxed.ai) for further details.
